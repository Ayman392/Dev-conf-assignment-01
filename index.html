When and where will DevConf 2026 take place?

DevConf 2026 will take place September 16–18, 2026, at Messe Berlin in Berlin, Germany. Check-in opens at 7:30 AM each day, with the opening keynote beginning at 9:00 AM on September 16.

How do I register for the conference?

Register online through the official DevConf 2026 website and choose your preferred pass and optional workshops. After payment, you’ll receive a confirmation email with your digital ticket and attendee portal access.

How much are tickets, and what is the refund policy?

Early-bird passes start at €399, standard passes at €549, and team discounts are available for five or more attendees. Refund requests received by July 31, 2026, are eligible for a refund minus a €40 processing fee; tickets may also be transferred until September 1.

When will the full conference schedule be available?

The complete agenda will be published in June 2026 and updated in the attendee portal and mobile app. You’ll be able to build a personal schedule and receive alerts if session details change.

Are speaker sessions and workshops included with my ticket?

Keynotes and breakout sessions are included with every conference pass. Hands-on workshops have limited capacity and may require a separate ticket, so advance booking is recommended.

What networking opportunities will be available?

Attendees can join topic-based meetups, mentor hours, community lounges, the welcome reception, and facilitated networking sessions. The event app also supports opt-in attendee matching and one-to-one meeting requests.

Will I receive a certificate of participation?

Yes. Verified attendees will receive a digital certificate by email within seven business days after the conference, and workshop completion badges will be issued separately where applicable.

Is discounted accommodation or travel support available?

DevConf has secured preferential rates at partner hotels near Messe Berlin, available through the attendee portal while rooms last. The travel guide also includes public transit directions, visa invitation letter requests, and accessibility information.

Can I watch DevConf 2026 online?

Keynotes and selected sessions will be streamed live for virtual pass holders, with recordings available on demand for 90 days. Workshops and some speaker sessions may remain in-person only due to licensing or participation requirements.

How can I contact the DevConf support team?

Email support@devconf2026.com or use live chat in the attendee portal, Monday–Friday, 9:00 AM–6:00 PM CET. During the event, visit the Help Desk in the main entrance hall for immediate assistance.
